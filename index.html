Module 01 — Level A1

DER NOMINATIV

The Subject Case — who or what performs the action

Grammar Rule

The Nominative case is used for the subject of a sentence — the person or thing performing the action. Definite articles: der (masc.), die (fem.), das (neut.), die (pl.). Indefinite articles: ein (masc./neut.), eine (fem.). Personal pronouns: ich, du, er, sie, es, wir, ihr, sie, Sie.
